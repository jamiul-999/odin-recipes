Dragon's Breath Steak Burger

Description

You want to make your own burger within only 10 minutes? Check this out!!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon diced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed garlic
  • 1 slices Gouda cheese
  • 1 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, onion, and garlic together in a large bowl. Shape into 4 patties.
  2. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Grill patties until browned, about 5 minutes. Flip and continue cooking on the second side, about 4 minutes. Add 1 slice of Gouda cheese to each patty; cook until slightly melted, about 1 minute. Serve on hamburger buns.
